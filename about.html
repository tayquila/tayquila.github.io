About TLMB!

Hello, I'm TL! For as long as I can remember music has been a significant part of my life. Music is more than sound, it's the way I see the world, the way I communicate, and the way I understand myself and others.

Through this website, I’ve poured pieces of what inspires me most into every page! I believe good music isn’t just heard, it’s felt and able to create connections. I wanted this site to be a glimpse into the songs, memories, and moods that have essentially shaped me into who I am!
